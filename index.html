No. Brooke catches the calls your team can't get to: after hours, weekends, the lunch break, and busy stretches when the phone goes unanswered. Your team still runs the practice, and Brooke just makes sure no caller is lost.

Does it work with my software?+

Brooke books into your Google Calendar and logs calls automatically, with nothing for your team to install or learn. We set up the connection for you.
