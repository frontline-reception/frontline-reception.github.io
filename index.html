FAQ

The questions clinics ask first.

Does Brooke replace my front desk?+

No. Brooke catches the calls your team can't get to — after hours, weekends, the lunch break, and busy stretches when the phone goes unanswered. Your team still runs the practice; Brooke just makes sure no caller is lost before they can.

Does it work with my software?+

Brooke books into your Google Calendar and logs calls automatically — there's nothing for your team to install or learn. We set up the connection for you.

How long does setup take?+

Days, not weeks. We tailor Brooke to your clinic — your name, hours, services and pricing — and you can hear her before she goes live.

Is there a contract?+

No annual lock-in. It's month-to-month — cancel any time with notice.

What about patient privacy?+

Brooke is built to respect the Australian Privacy Principles. Your bookings stay in your own Google tools, and call data is handled with care.
